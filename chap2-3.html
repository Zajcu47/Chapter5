Generalization

Generalization

Regular maintenance is crucial for preventing system failures and ensuring efficiency. It involves routine checks and repairs to prevent breakdowns, reduce repair costs, and maintain optimal performance. Neglecting maintenance can lead to significant challenges, including increased downtime and financial losses. Preventive maintenance is widely adopted in industries where reliability is key.
