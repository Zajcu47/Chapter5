Hello Students

Chapter 5: Maintain and Repair Computer Systems and Networks

In this chapter, you'll learn the key skills for maintaining and repairing computer systems and networks. From troubleshooting hardware to diagnosing software issues and performing preventive maintenance, we’ll cover everything you need to keep your technology running smoothly. Let’s get started!

Module 1: Diagnose Faults of Computer Systems and Networks

This lesson teaches how to diagnose and troubleshoot faults in computer systems and networks.

Module 2: Plan for Maintenance and Repair

This lesson covers planning effective maintenance and repair strategies to ensure the optimal performance and longevity of computer systems and networks.

Module 3: Rectify/Correct Defects in Computer Systems and Networks

This lesson focuses on identifying, correcting, and resolving defects in computer systems and networks to restore functionality and efficiency.

Module 4: Inspect and Test the Computer Systems and Networks

This lesson covers inspecting and testing computer systems and networks to ensure reliability, security, and optimal performance.

Module 5: Maintain Computer Systems and Networks

This lesson focuses on performing regular maintenance to ensure the efficiency, security, and longevity of computer systems and networks.
