Module 1: Diagnose faults for Computer Systems and Networks

Performance Task

Instructions: Watch the video and write an essay about its highlights, focusing on the parts that discuss diagnosing computer systems and networks.

Rubric:

Criteria Excellent (5 points) Good (4 points) Satisfactory (3 points) Needs Improvement (2 points) Unsatisfactory (1 point)
Content Understanding Thorough understanding of the video content; all key points are addressed. Good understanding; most key points are addressed. Basic understanding; some key points are missing. Poor understanding; many key points are missing. Little to no understanding of the content.
Clarity and Organization Essay is well-organized and clearly written; ideas flow logically. Generally organized; minor issues with clarity. Some organization; ideas may be unclear or disjointed. Poorly organized; difficult to follow. No clear organization; very difficult to understand.
Grammar and Mechanics No grammatical or spelling errors; polished writing. Few grammatical or spelling errors; generally polished. Some errors that do not significantly detract from the writing. Frequent errors that distract from the writing. Numerous errors that make the writing difficult to read.
Engagement with the Topic Demonstrates a high level of engagement and insight into the topic. Shows good engagement with the topic; some insights. Basic engagement; limited insights into the topic. Minimal engagement; lacks depth of thought. No engagement with the topic; lacks any insight.

Generalization

What is the first step you need to take to diagnose the issue, and how can you ensure you’re gathering all necessary information?

Are you carefully following each troubleshooting step without rushing, and how can you ensure you don’t skip important details?

How will you assess whether your solution has fully resolved the issue before concluding the troubleshooting process?
